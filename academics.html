PROGRAM STRUCTURE

Program Description

Our Bsc programs provides students with foundational knowledge and practical skills.
Students will engage in coursework, projects, and research designed to prepare them for
professional careers or advanced study.

Program Duration

Standard full-time duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
Part-time options available.

Total Credits Requirements

- Minimum total credits for graduation: 144 credits.
- Credits distributed across core, major, minor, and elective courses

Program Learning Outcomes

GRADUATES WILL BE ABLE TO:

  • Demonstrate comprehensive knowledge
  • Apply theoretical concepts to practical problems.
  • Communicate effectively in professional settings.
  • Engage in lifelong learning and ethical professional practice

ASSESSMENT METHOD

  1. Written exams
  2. Coursework and assignments
  3. Laboratory/practical work
  4. Group projects and presentations
  5. Final year project or thesis

Progression and Graduation Requirements

- Minimum cumulative GPA of 2.0
- Completion of all core and major requirements
- Successful defense of final year project
- Fulfillment of internship or practical training (where applicable)
