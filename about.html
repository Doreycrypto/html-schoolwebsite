Brief History

Founded in 1995, Evergreen Horizon University began as a small liberal arts college
with a commitment to academic excellence and community service. Over the years,
it has grown into a comprehensive university offering diverse programs in science,
technology, humanities, and business. Our dedication to innovation and inclusivity
has made us a beacon of learning and research in the region.

VISION

To be a globally recognized institution that inspires excellence, cultivates creativity,
and drives positive change for a sustainable future.

MISSION

To empower students through innovative education, cutting-edge research, and community engagement,
fostering lifelong learning and leadership in a diverse and inclusive environment.

VALUES

  1. Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical standards in all endeavors.
  2. Innovation: Encouraging creativity and embracing new ideas.
  3. Inclusivity: Promoting diversity and equal opportunities for all.
  4. Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in education and research.
  5. Community: Building strong partnerships and supporting societal growth.
